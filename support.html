MainDraw Support

Need help with your account, matches, tournaments, payments, or a safety concern? Contact us and we will get back to you as soon as possible.

Contact

Email us at support@maindraw.co.uk.

What to include

  • Your MainDraw account email.
  • The match, tournament, payment, or chat issue you need help with.
  • Screenshots or extra context if it helps us investigate.

Safety and disputes

For harassment, unsafe behaviour, no-shows, falsified scores, or payment issues, include the player name and match details so we can review the report.
